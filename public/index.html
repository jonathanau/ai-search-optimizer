Lumenyl is a free AI search readiness platform that benchmarks whether ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and Microsoft Copilot can discover, understand, trust, extract, and cite your website. It evaluates 30+ checks across 6 weighted dimensions—covering AI crawler access, server-rendered content, schema.org depth, answer-ready passages, citation authority, and emerging LLM standards—and returns a prioritized remediation roadmap in under 15 seconds.

Pro tip: analyze a strategic product, guide, or documentation page—not just the homepage.
